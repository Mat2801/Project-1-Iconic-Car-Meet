WARNING TO THE PUBLIC - MOTOR SPORT CAN BE DANGEROUS



Despite the organisers taking all reasonable precautions, unavoidable accidents can happen. Please comply with all instructions of marshals and notices and remain in permitted areas only. They are concerned with your safety.



Please observe the following rules to ensure that your time at all car shows and that of all our other visitors is safe and enjoyable.



Essential Information for Iconic Car Meets at all Events!



Driving On Site

10 MPH Speed Limit Everywhere on Site! Except for tracks and drag strips of course. Please watch your speed, there are many people walking around including young children. No quad bikes, mopeds or any other unlicensed motorised vehicles are allowed on site.



No parking on any roadway

Park only in the camping or parking areas and in accordance with the instructions of the parking marshals. No driving around the site after 9PM. No stunts, burnouts or donuts in roadways or campsites.



Ear Protection

Motorsport can be loud. Ear Protection is recommended when in close proximity to the track especially for young children. Ear Protection is available to purchase from most events



First Aid

Medical assistance can be found at the Medical Centre, in all locations please ask on arrival.



Security

Will be on site at all times throughout the events to ensure that you have a safe and enjoyable time. Their instructions must be followed at all times. Please contact them in any emergency.



Children On Site

Under 13's must be accompanied by an adult. Under 16's must be accompanied by an adult if staying on site over night.



Litter

Please dispose of your litter in the refuse sacks provided and/or in the clearly marked bins throughout all events.



Valuables

Please ensure all your valuables are carried with you or locked securely out of sight in your vehicle.


Restricted Areas

Never enter restricted areas, even if it looks safe.



For visitor safety and comfort

Raised viewing structures, tents or BBQ are not permitted on the spectator banking. Low level chairs are accepted but must NOT be used on any pathways or access points.



Fires & BBQs

Absolutely no open fires allowed. Only contained fires raised off the ground are permitted. This may include e.g. firepits, chimineas etc. BBQs are welcome but must be off the grass, on something safe and are only allowed in the campsites. After use let them cool properly and please dispose appropriately. If any fire or container is thought to present a risk of damage to the grass, then we may ask you to stop using it!



No Glass On Site

No glass bottles, jars etc allowed on site. This is for you and your car's safety and especially for those in the camping areas.



No Pets

No pets are allowed on site.



No Fireworks Or Chinese Lanterns

No Fireworks of any kind or Chinese Lanterns are not permitted on site. Chinese lanterns are a fire hazard.



No Trespassing

Do not trespass onto any of the adjoining private fields or woods.


No Independent Sound Systems

Independent sound systems are not allowed on site. Any music should be played at a reasonable level and all music should be switched off at 11PM.



No Drones

No unauthorised aerial drones of any kind are permitted over the site.



Drugs & Illegal Substances

Event owners do not condone the dealing in or use of illegal substances; if it’s illegal in the UK it’s also illegal within all car events. Event security will take an active role monitoring for drug dealing and will work in full co-operation with Police. Anyone found in possession of illegal substances or any other items which the organisers consider offensive or dangerous will have those items confiscated and the carrier will leave themselves liable for eviction from site. Anyone found in possession of larger quantities will be detained and dealt with by the police.



Site Safety

Outside of official event opening times the raceway is considered a construction site. Certain areas are dangerous, if you choose to leave the campsite or car park areas, you do so entirely at your own risk.



Arriving & Leaving

Please drive carefully through surrounding villages.
